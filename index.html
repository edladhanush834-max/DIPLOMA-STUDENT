Voice Commands Cheat Sheet

"go to dashboard" Switches to your student dashboard view.
"go to prep" / "go to syllabus" Switches view to the Active Branch study resources.
"go to money" / "go to budget" Switches view to the expense ledger.
"read notes" Reads aloud all study notes for the active subject.
"add expense [amount] for [description]" Logs spending. Example: "Add expense 150 for book purchase".
"reset budget" Resets the monthly allowance and expense ledger.
